Something unspeakable happened in the middle of a cornfield 2 years ago...

Now, at last, each of the residents of Milton, Nebraska are ready to tell their own sides of the story.

Alex Lubisher's Off-Broadway hit is back for the second time in New York since it's debut to re-explore how a small town dealt with the weight of tragedy in ways both light and dark.

Experience the tragedy and comedy of Milton through the raw, intimate direction of rising director Justin Cimino.
