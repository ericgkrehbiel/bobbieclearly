Something unspeakable happened in the middle of a cornfield 2 years ago...

Now, at last, each of the residents of Milton, Nebraska are ready to tell their own sides of the story.

Written by Alex Lubischer, Bobbie Clearly is back by demand for the second time in New York since it's Off-Broadway debut to re-explore how a small town dealt with the weight of tragedy in ways both light and dark.

Experience the tragedy and comedy of Milton through the raw, intimate direction of Justin Cimino.
